*Limitations->

->Hallucinations (Generating Incorrect Information)-
Anthropic's own help center acknowledges that Claude may produce incorrect or misleading
 responses, including fabricated citations and outdated information. The company explicitly
 warns users not to rely on Claude as the sole source of truth for high-stakes decisions.
->Less Reliable for Real-Time Information-
Anthropic states that Claude may become confused when asked about current events because 
it may not have been trained on the latest information.
->Sometimes Overly Cautious or Refuses Benign Requests-
Anthropic's Constitution explicitly discusses the risk of Claude being "overly cautious" and refusing 
reasonable requests. The company acknowledges that excessive refusals reduce usefulness.
->Mathematical and Logical Errors Can Still Occur-
Recent research shows that language models can sometimes perform correct reasoning steps but still 
produce incorrect final answers. Claude models were observed making such mistakes on specialized 
reasoning benchmarks.
->Safety Restrictions May Limit Flexibility-
Claude prioritizes safety and responsible AI use.
